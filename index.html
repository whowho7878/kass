00 — The Origin

Roots
in
Monga.

A contemporary retail concept space, co-created by members of the Huang Ju-Yi family — residents of Monga since 1843 — in collaboration with the esteemed British audio brand Void Acoustics and the venerable artisanal bakery, Bread Kitchen.

Curator A(h)-WAN has lived and grown amidst the vibrant streets of Ximending. His profound connection to this neighbourhood inspired him to establish a unique space here — celebrating the pride of being a native of Monga.
